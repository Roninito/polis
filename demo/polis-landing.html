The Process

From idea to governed organization
in minutes.

Answer a few questions. AI drafts your constitution, laws, and member structure. Launch. Every decision made afterward is logged, transparent, and enforced by the same rules you wrote.

01
🎯
Define Your Organization
Choose your org type — family trust, town, savings circle, church, business, or community. Our AI founding wizard interviews you about your purpose, members, finances, and values.
02
📜
AI Drafts Your Charter
The AI generates a complete constitution, operating laws, member registry, and treasury structure based on your interview. Every word is yours to review and edit before ratification.
03
⚖️
Govern by the Rules You Set
Members propose laws, vote, manage funds, and review decisions — all within your constitution. The AI enforces the rules impartially. It can't override member votes. Ever.
04
🔍
Full Transparency Always
Every AI decision is logged in the SAR Activity Log with its full reasoning — what it saw, what it analyzed, what it did. The ledger is immutable. The records are permanent.
